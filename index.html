Welcome to Fahad Dankabo Foundation

The Fahad Dankabo Foundation (FDF) is a philanthropic and social entrepreneurial initiative founded with a mission to uplift vulnerable and underserved communities in Kano State, Nigeria, and beyond. We are committed to fostering sustainable development through humanitarian services, youth mobilization, and women empowerment.

About Us

Mission: To foster sustainable social and economic development by providing financial, educational, and moral support to vulnerable communities, empowering women and youth, and advocating for inclusive growth with special emphasis on education and rural development.

Vision: To be a leading philanthropic organization in Northern Nigeria, creating lasting impacts in vulnerable communities by addressing pressing socio-economic challenges, promoting education, and empowering marginalized groups.

Our Purpose

The Fahad Dankabo Foundation strives to:

  • Support education by funding student registration fees and advocating for girl-child education.
  • Alleviate poverty by supporting vulnerable households, especially during Ramadan.
  • Empower women and youth through skill acquisition and entrepreneurship programs.
  • Promote community development by mobilizing youth and collaborating with elders.

Programmes & Initiatives

  • Educational Support Programme: Providing scholarships, funding registration fees, and promoting girl-child education.
  • Ramadan Financial Aid Programme: Providing financial aid to vulnerable families during Ramadan for food, clothing, and healthcare.
  • Youth Mobilization and Empowerment Programme: Offering entrepreneurship training and mentorship to youths.
  • Girl Child Education Initiative: Advocating for girl-child enrollment and addressing cultural barriers to education.
  • Rural Women Empowerment Programme: Offering skill acquisition training and micro-loans for rural women.
  • Community Development Projects: Building schools, clinics, and clean water wells through collaboration with elders.

Organizational Structure

  • Board of Trustees: Oversees the foundation's strategic direction and alignment with its mission and vision.
  • Executive Director: Manages daily operations and ensures program efficiency.
  • Program Managers: Oversee specific programs such as education, youth mobilization, and women empowerment.
  • Monitoring & Evaluation Team: Assesses the impact of programs and ensures goals are met.
  • Volunteers: Assist in implementing various programs and initiatives.
